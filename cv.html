Curriculum Vitae

Academic profile, telescope programs, publications, teaching, and outreach

I am an extragalactic astronomer working on galaxy formation and evolution across cosmic time, with a focus on dust-obscured star formation, high-redshift galaxies, and multi-wavelength observations.

My research combines JWST imaging, ALMA and NOEMA interferometry, VLA radio data, and spectral-energy-distribution modelling to trace how galaxies assemble stars, dust, and gas from cosmic noon toward the epoch of reionization.
