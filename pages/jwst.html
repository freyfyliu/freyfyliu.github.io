JWST

NIRCam and NIRSpec views of high-redshift galaxies

JWST provides the rest-frame ultraviolet and optical view needed to identify high-redshift galaxies, characterize stellar populations, and interpret dust-obscured activity in context.

I use JWST datasets in combination with ALMA, VLA, HST, VISTA, and ground-based spectroscopy to build a multi-wavelength view of galaxy evolution.
