Obscured Star Formation History

the hidden star-formation budget back to z ~ 7

A major part of galaxy growth is hidden from optical surveys. By combining JWST-selected samples with ALMA continuum detections and stacking analyses, I quantify the dust-obscured contribution to cosmic star formation.

The goal is to connect individual faint millimeter sources to population-level histories without losing the physics of gas, dust, and stellar mass assembly.
