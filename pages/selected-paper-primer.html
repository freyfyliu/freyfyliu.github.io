JWST PRIMER + ALMA

dust-obscured star formation in the COSMOS field

This project connects deep JWST imaging with ALMA-detected galaxies to study dusty star formation over a large fraction of cosmic history.

The central question is how much star formation is missed by rest-frame ultraviolet surveys and how that missing component evolves with redshift.
